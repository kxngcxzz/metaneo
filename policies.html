Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2025

Who We Are

Meta Neo is a digital education brand operated by Casey Mungofa, based in the United Kingdom. We provide digital products, educational content, and consulting services relating to social media growth and monetisation. Our website is metaneo.com.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What Data We Collect

  • Email address — when you sign up to receive our free guide or join our email list
  • Name — when you complete a contact or booking form
  • Instagram handle — optionally provided when booking a discovery call
  • Payment information — processed securely by Whop; Meta Neo does not store card details
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics about how visitors use our website

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent — when you sign up to our email list
  • Contractual necessity — when you purchase a product
  • Legitimate interests — for improving our services and website analytics

Your Rights Under UK GDPR

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent at any time (e.g. unsubscribe from emails)
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of your data rights, email us at hello@metaneo.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by UK GDPR.
